<p>Evergreen Secondary School is committed to helping our students succeed
in school and beyond. Here are some of the notable achievements of our
EVG Alumni.</p>
<h4><strong>Year 2025:</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Celebrating SG60: EVG Alumni at the National Day Parade</strong>
</p>
<p>Evergreen Secondary is proud to celebrate two of our alumni, Ms Stella
Tay and Mr Muhammad Khairi Khairudin, who were both in the video featurette
shown during the mobile column at the National Day Parade SG60 celebrations
[<a href="https://www.youtube.com/live/u1RxF6f4qyk?si=2fqmTlNhvbnxOmWq&amp;t=6775" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">1</a>],
[<a href="https://www.youtube.com/live/u1RxF6f4qyk?si=9pSDZT3hXszQ4YGh&amp;t=6739" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">2</a>].</p>
<p>Additionally, Deputy Superintendent of Police Stella Tay is featured in
The Straits Times [<a href="https://www.straitstimes.com/singapore/father-and-son-to-lead-ndp-2025-mobile-column-to-celebrate-commitment-to-defence-across-generations" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">ST article</a>].</p>
<p>Second Warrant Officer Muhammad Khairi Khairudin is featured with his
father and brother in Berita Harian [<a href="https://www.beritaharian.sg/singapura/satu-hati-lawan-api-keluarga-bomba-bersama-jayakan-ndp-2025" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">BH article</a>]
for his family’s strong dedication to service in the SCDF.</p>
<p>Their participation at the National Day Parade are a testament to their
commitment, resilience, and service to our nation.</p>
<p>Their achievements also reflect Evergreen Secondary’s long-standing focus
on National Education, which has shaped generations of students with a
deep sense of rootedness, responsibility, and pride in Singapore.</p>
<p>We are inspired by Stella and Khairi’s journeys, and their stories remind
us of the strong values nurtured at Evergreen Secondary—values that continue
to guide our alumni as they make a difference in the community and beyond.</p>
