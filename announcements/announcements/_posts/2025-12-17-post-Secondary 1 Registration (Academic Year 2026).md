<h4><strong>1. Secondary 1 Online Briefing on 22 December 2025</strong></h4>
<p></p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
We will be having a <u>MS Teams session to brief all Secondary 1 students and parents on 22 December 2025 at 8.30am</u>.
The MS Teams link details can be obtained through Parents Gateway.</p>
<p></p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
Please contact our General Office at 63656392 between 8.00am to 4.00pm
on weekdays if you have any queries regarding accessing the MS Teams session.</p>
<p></p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
When joining the MS Teams session, please use the official name (as per
NRIC) of your child being admitted to our school, e.g., “John Tan Lee Beng”.
The MS Teams session will open at 8.15am. You will be placed in the virtual
waiting room, for admission into the session at 8.30am.</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
We would appreciate it if only one MS Teams account per child and your
child’s official name are used for the signing in.</p>
